## Description

Join us in a hands-on lab to create your data catalog. This lab will include Data Map, Unified Catalog and a lab for Data Security.

**Cataloging:**
- Ingesting metadata
- Curating and creating business concepts

**Data Health:**
- Configuring rules, scheduling, profiling and scans
- Applying controls and viewing reports
- Exporting metadata to Fabric

**Workflows:**
- Customizing access request and curation workflows

**Data Security:**
- Applying MIP labels and DLP policies
- Setting up IRM and DSPM

## Prerequisites

- Understanding of data governance concepts
- Familiarity with Microsoft Purview helpful
