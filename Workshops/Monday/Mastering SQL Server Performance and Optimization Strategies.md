## Description

Everyone wants their database to go faster, whether it's shaving hours off your ETL process, taking minutes off the runtime for reports, or trimming milliseconds off a critical OLTP query.

Every database professional must know where to look and how to tune, whether you have one SQL environment or thousands.

In this full-day session, you'll learn how to take your performance tuning skills to the next level.
