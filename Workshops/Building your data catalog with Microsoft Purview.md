## Description

Join us in a hands-on lab to create your data catalog. This lab will include Data Map, Unified Catalog and a lab for Data Security.



Cataloging

1. Ingesting metadata 

2. Curating and creating business concepts



Data Health

1. Configuring rules, scheduling, profiling and scans

2. Applying controls and viewing reports

3. Exporting metadata to Fabric 



Workflows

1. Customizing access request and curation workflows



Data Security:

1. Applying MIP labels and DLP policies

2. Setting up IRM and DSPM 



*Please note that this workshop is hands-on and requires attendees to bring a laptop to participate.
