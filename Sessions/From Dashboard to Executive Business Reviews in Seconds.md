## Description

It's time to fix the "Last Mile" of your data journey by automating your recurring reporting. In this fast-paced 20-minute session, discover how to automate the tedious translation of Power BI dashboards into thousands of tailored executive-ready slide decks that you can share with leadership or customers.

What you'll learn:
- Eliminate Manual Grunt Work: How to set up "set it and forget it" automation for recurring reports (QBRs, Client Updates, Financial Decks)
- Preserve Data Integrity: Stop worrying about version control errors or copy-paste mistakes; keep your slides in perfect sync with your semantic models
- Scale Your Storytelling: How to instantly generate hundreds of personalized deck variations (e.g., one unique deck per region or client) from a single Power BI report
