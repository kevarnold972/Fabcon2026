## About

Anita is a Product Manager at Microsoft where she works on Power BI. She previously worked on the Fabric Customer Advisory Team leading private preview engagements and customer evidence across Fabric workloads. She holds a degree in Computer Science from Purdue University. Prior to Microsoft, she was an intern for Salesforce and the United States Department of Defense.
