## About

Noah Mattern is a Director and Market Leader in BDO Digital’s Data & AI practice in the Oak Brook IL office with more than 25 years of experience providing data analytics and technology advisory services. Noah often works with clients to assess existing capabilities and create an action plan or roadmap to allow the organization to gain material value from key insights and knowledge.​
Noah is familiar with many technologies including on-premise and cloud, dashboarding and visualizations, predictive and Generative AI frequently designing solutions which leverage these technologies to turn data into information upon which key business decisions are made. ​
Noah enjoys working with clients to first understand the business’s core values, culture, and key drivers.  From this base, solutions can be created which address the right concerns in the right way, and to the right degree.
