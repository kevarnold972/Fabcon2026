## About

Sam Kelly is a Fabric and Power BI enthusiast with over eight years of experience working with Power BI and the latest advancements in Microsoft Fabric. He is passionate about solution design utilizing Fabric and Power BI, as well as general governance design for the Power BI platform. He has been a part of building multiple communities of Power BI enthusiasts, including global centers of excellence and casual knowledge sharing groups.
