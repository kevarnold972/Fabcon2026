## About

Emily Lisa is a Principal Product Manager on the Microsoft Power BI team, driving features that empower Power BI creators to get more from their semantic models, visualizations, and integrations across the Microsoft Fabric ecosystem. Emily is passionate about democratizing analytics and making semantic modeling accessible to everyone. Outside of work, Emily enjoys scuba diving and volunteers at the Seattle Aquarium.
