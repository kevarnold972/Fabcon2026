## About

Bradley Schacht is a Principal Program Manager on the Microsoft Fabric product team based in Jacksonville, FL. Bradley is a former consultant, trainer, and has authored 6 SQL Server and Power BI books, most recently Learn Microsoft Fabric. As a member of the Microsoft Fabric product team, Bradley works directly with customers to solve some of their most complex data problems and helps shape the future of Microsoft Fabric. Bradley regularly speaks at events such as the Fabric Community Conference, SQLBits, Future Data Driven Summit, PASS Summit, SQL Saturdays, Code Camps, and user groups across the country including locally at the Jax Data User Group. He is a contributor on SQLServerCentral.com and blogs on his personal site, BradleySchacht.com.
