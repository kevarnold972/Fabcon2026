## About

Jared Brown brings over 20 years of industry experience, with the past 10 focused on Microsoft technologies.  Jared has been champion for Power BI since its release, was recognized as a FastTrack solution architect, and is the leader of the Hartford Power BI User Group.  One of his many roles at EY includes Microsoft Fabric enablement through our Global Fabric Center of Excellence, which aligns perfectly with his affinity for sharing knowledge with the larger Microsoft community!
