## About

Vishnu Vasanth is Co-Founder and CEO of e6data, a next-generation lakehouse compute engine built for high-concurrency analytics and complex SQL and AI workloads that delivers dramatically faster performance and lower total cost of ownership. 

With over 20 years of experience across technology, finance, investment, and go-to-market leadership, Vishnu has built and scaled data-centric businesses across industries. Prior to e6data, he held leadership roles including Director of Investments at Equicap Asia and co-founder of RenewGEN.
