## About

A data-driven architect with deep expertise in building scalable analytics environments from the ground up. With a strong foundation in Python, SQL, DAX, and Azure technologies, Curtis has led cross-functional teams to deliver high-impact financial and operational insights. He architected and scaled a CI/CD-enabled analytics platform supporting 30+ developers, producing executive-level dashboards for companies with annual revenues as large as $9 billion. His startup-relevant experience includes designing lean data models, optimizing DevOps workflows, and aligning analytics with business strategy. Curtis also brings leadership experience. He is the creator and maintainer of PyTabular, a Python package designed to streamline tabular data modeling and analytics workflows, demonstrating his commitment to building efficient, developer-friendly tools. He thrives in environments that demand agility, innovation, and end-to-end ownership of analytics solutions.
