## About

Lauren Faber is Principal Product Manager for Power BI working on Power BI Copilot. She received her master's in information systems management from Brigham Young University in 2018. Prior to joining Microsoft, she interned at Walmart Labs and a start-up as a software engineer. She was initially a member of the Power BI Customer Advisory Team working with customers to showcase their journeys with Power BI, and lead customer Power BI events to gather information on customer needs around the globe.
