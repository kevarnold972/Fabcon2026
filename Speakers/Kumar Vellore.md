## About

Kumar Vellore is the Managing Director of Data & AI at LevelShift, where he drives innovation through data modernization and AI-powered solutions. Kumar has been at the forefront of implementing secure data-driven solutions, including intelligent agents that revolutionize decision-making and customer personalization. Recently, he led the transformation of a leading retail giant by modernizing its data platform and integrating predictive AI models to enhance operational efficiency and customer experience. With a wealth of expertise in enterprise data solutions, Kumar is passionate about enabling businesses to achieve agility, efficiency, and competitive advantage through advanced analytics and intelligent systems. His leadership and insights have empowered organizations to make informed decisions, drive innovation, and achieve scalable growth.
