## About

Younes Jallouli is the Co-Founder and Chief Product Officer at Rollstack, a platform that automates the creation of data-driven slide decks and documents. At Rollstack, Younes focuses on solving the "last mile" of analytics by helping teams seamlessly inject governed data into their workflows. Prior to founding his own company, he spent four years bridging engineering and product roles at SlimPay and worked as a Systems Integration Engineer at Tesla. Younes holds a degree from ENSEEIHT and is an active member of the Y Combinator community.
