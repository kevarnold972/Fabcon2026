## About

Aditya Badramraju is a Principal Program Manager with the AzureSQL team, specializing in Azure SQL Hyperscale. Before joining Microsoft, Aditya worked as a Data Platform Architect at a renowned financial firm, where they implemented SQL Servers and various data-related technologies. Aditya is a passionate data enthusiast with a deep love for all things computer science. He thrives on solving complex customer problems and have a strong passion for speaking and sharing knowledge about data and technology.
