## About

Philipp Kiencke works as the Global Partner Manager at dab GmbH, supporting customers and partners worldwide in projects involving the utilization of data from SAP systems. His main goal is to make the data available to everyone and to facilitate access, regardless of whether IT knowledge is available or specialist departments require specific analyses.
