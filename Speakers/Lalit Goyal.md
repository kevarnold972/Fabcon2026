## About

Lalit Goyal is a senior Data & AI leader with over 25 years of global experience helping enterprises modernize their data platforms and unlock business value through analytics, cloud, and AI. 

He specializes in architecting modern data platforms on Microsoft Fabric, Azure, Databricks, AWS, and Snowflake, with deep expertise in Lakehouse modernization, real-time analytics, data governance, and AI-driven automation. Lalit has led complex migrations from legacy systems—including Synapse, ADF, SAP, Oracle, and 50+ ERP environments—into unified, governed, AI-ready architectures.

A frequent advisor to Fortune 500 organizations, focuses on building scalable data products, operationalizing AI with Copilot and Data Agents, and establishing domain-driven, governed analytics foundations. Passionate about innovation and community learning, he actively contributes thought leadership on modern data strategy, AI adoption, and cloud transformation.
