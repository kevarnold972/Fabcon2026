## About

Amir Sarir is a Full-Stack Senior Software Engineer at Motio, leading efforts to implement automated and compliance testing solutions for Analytics environments. As a Technical Product Manager, Amir spearheaded the development of a modern Angular-based UI and an ElasticSearch-powered search solution that significantly enhanced navigation. He also founded Motio’s first Customer Advisory Panel to align the product roadmap with strategic customer needs, driving adoption, engagement, and long-term value across Motio’s analytics platform. In his current position as a BI and AI Solutions Engineer, he focuses on researching and developing innovative solutions for Power BI environments. He identifies key pain points and designs tools to enhance performance, reliability, and user experience, creating an invigorating DevOps experience for an invincible Power BI.
