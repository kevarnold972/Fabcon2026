## About

Kumar Thangamuthu is a Senior Product Manager at SAS with over 15 years of experience building and launching enterprise-scale SaaS solutions. He leads the product strategy for a portfolio of AI-driven products, specializing in decision intelligence. His expertise lies in orchestrating AI models and business logic within complex decision workflows, helping large organizations automate their most critical operations. Kumar holds an MBA from Boston University and an MS in Computer Science.
