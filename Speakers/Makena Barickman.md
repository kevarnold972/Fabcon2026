## About

Makena is a Product Manager on the SQL Experiences team with a focus on all things SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS). She is passionate about design, creating fantastic user experiences, and accessibility. Her previous experience includes user-experience focused growth product management and accessibility program management at Zillow. When she's not working, you can find her hitting the trails near her home in the PNW, testing out a new recipe, or traveling the globe with her husband.
