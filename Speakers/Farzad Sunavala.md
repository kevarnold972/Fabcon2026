## About

Farzad is a Principal Product Manager at Microsoft, leading Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) capabilities on Azure AI Search. He is dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations to create innovative tech experiences. Before Microsoft, Farzad held various engineering and product management roles at Chevron Corporation. He holds a B.S. in Petroleum Engineering from Louisiana State University, with minors in Computer Science and Technical Sales, and an M.Eng. in Engineering Management from Cornell University.
