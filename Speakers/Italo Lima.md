## About

Italo Lima is a Senior Staff Data Scientist with extensive experience across leading tech companies. At Facebook, Italo worked in infrastructure, focusing on forecasting usage and compute growth. His tenure at Netflix saw him optimizing quality of service and enhancing user experience through precise forecasting. At Pinterest, Italo built and led a data science team dedicated to infrastructure and cost engineering. His work at Google centered on growth and AI for the Gmail platform. Currently, at Microsoft Real Time Intelligence, Italo continues leveraging his vast expertise to deliver innovative solutions.
