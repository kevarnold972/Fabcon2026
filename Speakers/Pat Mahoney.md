## About

Pat Mahoney is a former Data Platform MVP and Community Super User for Power BI, Power Apps, and Power Automate. After being a citizen developer in the pharma industry for a long time, he is now a member of the Microsoft Fabric CAT team. He also has a blog and YouTube channel under the name of HoosierBI with content focused on Power Query, DAX/modeling, and custom visuals.
