## About

Deepa is a Senior Program Manager driving AI-assisted experiences in Microsoft Fabric Notebooks. She focuses on making Copilot in Notebooks intuitive, predictable, and supportive by designing clear, trustworthy agentic workflows that are accessible to users across skill levels. Her work centers on simplifying complex interactions so people can move faster and with greater confidence. Passionate about grounding AI innovation in real customer needs, she is dedicated to elevating the everyday experience of teams working in Fabric Notebooks.
