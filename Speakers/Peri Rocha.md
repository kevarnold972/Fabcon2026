## About

Peri is a Product Manager with 27 years in analytics, and a diverse set of experiences in the software industry across product engineering, sales, business programs and marketing. He has lived and worked in three continents, which helped him build a diverse cultural background. He is the author of "Learn Azure Synapse Data Explorer", published by Packt Publishing. 

On his personal interests, Peri is  a recording artist with a deep passion for writing and performing music. He also enjoys studying and practicing karate, and is an avid reader.
