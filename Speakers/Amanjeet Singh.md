## About

Amanjeet has been an employee of Microsoft for over six years, and he is currently a principal in Fabric customer advisory team. He works directly with customers who are pushing the scale and performance boundaries of Microsoft Fabric. In his role, he is responsible in helping customers designing Fabric architecture and addressing technical roadblocks. He also works closely with Fabric OneLake product group in driving feedback; prioritising customer asks; and influencing product roadmap.

Prior to joining Fabric CAT, Amanjeet was a senior architect in the team which published prescriptive guidance for deploying landing zones on Azure; well-architected framework; and Enterprise scale analytics.

Before joining Microsoft, in a career spanning 14 years, he held various technical positions at Woolworths (Australia) and Insurance Australia Group (Australia).
