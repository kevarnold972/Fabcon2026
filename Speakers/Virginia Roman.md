## About

Virginia Roman is a Product Manager on the Microsoft Fabric Data Science and AI team. She has a background in computer science and six years of experience at Microsoft, ranging from automating data collection in the network infrastructure space, to building AI features in Microsoft Office, to now empowering Fabric users to adopt AI to streamline their workflows.
