## About

Srikumar Nair is a seasoned product leader with over two decades of experience driving innovation and scaling enterprise‑grade B2B SaaS solutions. With a foundation in product engineering—spanning developer, architect, and presales—he pairs deep technical expertise with strategic vision to deliver transformative products. He has shaped flagship offerings across Windows, Microsoft 365 (including SharePoint Online), Enterprise Mobility, Dynamics 365, and Power Platform. As a Principal Group Product Manager at Microsoft, Srikumar leads product strategy for Agent 365, the enterprise control plane for AI agents, contributing to the key value pillars of Agent 365 like registry, access control, interoperability, and security. He focuses on building agentic tools to connect AI agents with enterprise knowledge and IQ besides ensuring real‑time observability, protection and developer tools like the SDKs and MCP servers across Work IQ, Foundry IQ and Fabric IQ to make agents enterprise‑ready.
