## About

David Levy is a Principal Program Manager at Microsoft, driving SQL connectivity strategy and developer experience across Azure SQL Database, SQL Server, and Microsoft Fabric. With deep expertise in cross-platform tooling and language drivers, David leads initiatives that simplify database development for modern workloads, including AI integration and cloud-native applications. A frequent speaker at community events, David is passionate about empowering developers with practical guidance, innovative tools, and best practices to build scalable, secure solutions.
