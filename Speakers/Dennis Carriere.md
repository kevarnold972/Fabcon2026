## About

Dennis is the Head of Client Success at Collectiv, a Microsoft Solutions Partner in Data & AI and Azure with a Specialization in Analytics, a Fabric Featured Partner, and a Certified Databricks Consulting Partner. With over a decade of experience designing and implementing large-scale data solutions, Dennis has a proven track record of architecting robust, scalable data platforms that drive business transformation. His expertise spans data architecture, planning and forecasting solutions, AI integration, and advanced analytics across the Microsoft ecosystem. Dennis has led numerous high-impact projects leveraging Fabric, Databricks, Copilot, Power BI, and Azure to deliver intelligent, real-time insights that power smarter decision-making. A graduate of Louisiana State University, Dennis is passionate about helping organizations eliminate silos, modernize planning processes, and unlock the full potential of their data through innovation and automation.
