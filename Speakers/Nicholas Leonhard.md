## About

Nicholas Leonhard is a Machine Learning Engineer responsible for building and maintaining modern MLOps systems in Microsoft Fabric. He leads the development of end-to-end machine-learning pipelines that support student success, enrollment forecasting, and operational decision-making. Nicholas specializes in traditional ML and works extensively with Python, pandas, PySpark, scikit-learn, and MLflow. He holds a B.S. in Computer Science and an M.S. in Data Science & Analytics with an emphasis in High Performance Computing.
