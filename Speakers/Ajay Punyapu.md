## About

Ajay brings over 22 years of experience as a Solutions Architect, specializing in designing and implementing on-premise and cloud-based solutions for ETL, database management, security, data warehousing, data strategy, and reporting across various industries. He has presented at Fabric and Microsoft Dynamics Community Conferences and has co-presented in webcasts and TSI events in collaboration with Microsoft. Ajay is also a member of the Fabric Partner Voice program, getting access to test and evaluate Private Preview features and share feedback, insights with Microsoft/Community and identify and implement potential use cases where applicable.
