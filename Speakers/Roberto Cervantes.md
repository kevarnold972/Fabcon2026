## About

Roberto has 18 years of experience in the software industry, he is a Sr. Product Manager in the Azure Messaging team, specialized in designing, architecting and developing enterprise applications.

In 2012, he joined Microsoft, in the last years he has been focused on developing cloud native solutions based on a wide stack of technologies for app innovation, microservices, serverless, messaging and containers. Roberto has been working across multiple organizations: Microsoft Consulting Services, Developer Experience (DX) - Visual Studio, Xamarin, Global Black Belts and Azure Event Grid, where he plays a key role enhancing the service's scalability and reliability, enabling event-driven features and capabilities for hundreds of customers around the globe.
