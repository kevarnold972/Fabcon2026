## About

Nathan is the Vice President of Innovation at a leading data, analytics, and AI consulting firm, where he helps organizations modernize their data and analytics ecosystems. He leads the team behind Vortex, an automated migration platform that accelerates the move from Qlik, Tableau, and Cognos into Power BI and Microsoft Fabric. Nathan focuses on solving real-world migration challenges with scalable patterns that reduce complexity, shorten timelines, and unlock the full value of Fabric. He has partnered with dozens of companies across all industries and sizes, helping teams navigate modernization with clarity and confidence.
