## About

Thomas LeBlanc is a business intelligence architect, trainer and author. He has been building performant data warehouse solutions for the past 20+ years has certifications in Power BI, Fabric, Dimensional Modeling and Data Vault 2.0. He graduated from LSU in Quantitative Business Analysis with a concentration in Management Information Systems. Microsoft awarded him Data Platform MVP in 2017 and has been renewed every year since.
He has focused on data warehouse solutions(on-premises and Azure) using tools like SSIS, SSAS, Power BI, Synapse, Fabric and Data Factory. He recently published the 2nd edition Power BI Performance Best Practices.
Involved in the data community since 2012, he helps the Baton Rouge User Groups and hosting SQLSaturday. Has spoken at PASS Summit, SQLSaturday, Live360, VSLive, Dynamics Community and Power Platform. 
Visit his website at Thomas-LeBlanc.com, articles on LinkedIn (https://www.linkedin.com/in/thomaslleblanc/) or follow him on Bluesky (@PowerBIDude).
