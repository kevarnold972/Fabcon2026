## About

Minni is a Principal Program Manager with the Microsoft Fabric Customer Advisory Team (CAT), specializing in Real-Time Intelligence capabilities within Fabric. With over 20 years in tech, she has extensive experience in developing applications and data platforms. She spent more than a decade as an Enterprise Architect before transitioning into data analytics and engineering roles at Microsoft. She is a blogger, speaker and Microsoft Certified Trainer.
