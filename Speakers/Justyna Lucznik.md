## About

Justyna leads the Product Management team for Microsoft Fabric Data Engineering, Data Science, and AI, covering Lakehouse, notebooks, Spark jobs, models & experiments, and Fabric Data Agents. Her team empowers data engineers to build and scale Lakehouse architectures and enables data scientists to train and operationalize machine learning models. Finally with Data Agents, data analysts can build AI powered virtual analysts on top of all their organizational data, to explore data and uncover insights. Before joining Fabric, Justyna led the Power BI AI team.
