## About

Haydn Richardson is a Principal Group Program Manager at Microsoft, where Haydn leads the Microsoft Fabric Shell and Growth team responsible for both the Fabric web application experience and Fabric product lifecycle experiences across Free, Trial and Paid. With over two decades of experience building products that inspire data-driven decision-making, from SQL Server to Power BI to Microsoft Fabric, Haydn is passionate about technology that solves real-world problems and delivers measurable impact. Outside of work, Haydn enjoys cycling, sailing, and exploring self-propelled outdoor adventures.
