## About

Michael Destein is the Vice President of Technology Alliances at Quantexa, leading global ecosystem strategy and partnerships that accelerate innovation through collaboration. He plays a pivotal role in advancing Quantexa Unify, a new joint initiative with Microsoft that brings contextual decision intelligence to Microsoft Fabric. With over 20 years of experience in data, analytics, and alliance leadership, Michael helps organizations bridge fragmented data to create clarity, trust, and smarter decisions. His focus on partnership-driven transformation enables enterprises to operationalize AI-powered context at scale, driving measurable business outcomes and demonstrating how collaboration between Quantexa and Microsoft is shaping the future of intelligent data unification.
