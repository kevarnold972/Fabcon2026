## About

Hasan is a Senior Product Manager at Microsoft Fabric, leading te work on AI-automation, Fabric MCP Servers and the Fabric CLI.

With over 15 years of experience across startups, corporates, and social enterprises, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and a passion for innovation and social impact.

Recognized on Forbes' 30 Under 30 list, Hasan has founded and led multiple initiatives that raised millions to foster entrepreneurship, STEAM education and economic growth for underrepresented communities, driving diversity and inclusive prosperity.
