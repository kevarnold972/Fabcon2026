## About

Blazing the software solutions trail at Motio, VP of Business Development Edwin van Megesen is no stranger to the world of analytics. His previous experience as a manager at PriceWaterhouseCoopers served not only as a strong foundation to bolster his versatility and knowledge but as the spark that would ignite his passion for helping others.

Whether it’s supporting customers by helping them meet internal and external regulatory requirements, exploring analytics solutions to help organizations save time and money, or caring for the environment and humanitarian causes, Edwin approaches everything he does wholeheartedly. 

From previously lecturing at the University of Twente, Hogeschool in Holland, and the Intercontinental University of Curaçao (ICUC) to saving the world, Edwin works tirelessly in all he does. He is an expert on the Analytic lifecycle development process that includes CI/CD and DevOps for Power BI. He brings this passion to organizations so they can implement streamlined work processes supported by automation, which, in turn, provides guardrails for a successful PowerBI implementation.
