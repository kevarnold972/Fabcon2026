## About

Tino is the Director of Product for Fabric Data Warehouse. In the past, Tino spent nearly a decade building Google BigQuery and co-founded MotherDuck, a database startup funded by a16z, Redpoint, Altimeter, and Felicis. When not at work, Tino spends time with his family and doing various outdoors activities like road cycling, gravel cycling, and other types of cycling. His key to success is drinking copious amounts of espresso.
