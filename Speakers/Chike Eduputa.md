## About

Chike is an expert in business and technology transformation, with over a decade of experience leading global teams deliver large-scale, high-impact programmes. He combines Microsoft Solution Architecture, Agile Delivery and Product Management to deliver results on client projects across multiple sectors. Chike advises business and technology leaders on latest trends, the impact on organisational design, product design and development, and portfolio management. As an intrapreneur, he develops new digital products and services and leads the Capgemini Invent UK Microsoft Advisory team.
