## About

Ajay is a Data & AI Expert with 24 years of Industry knowledge in Data Warehousing, Data Analytics, and Machine Learning. Prior to his role as Azure Solutions Director at Informatica, he was working as Practice Leader with Accenture on Azure Data & AI and as an Architect at Microsoft and has also contributed to multiple patents in data engineering. Ajay provides leadership and mentoring to multi-disciplinary teams of engineers, modelers, and data scientists. He is passionate about data architecture and contributes to industry knowledge on Data to drive the next generation of Analytics. He is also a regular speaker at the NOVA SQL user group and evangelizes best practices on data architecture and decentralized technology solutions.
