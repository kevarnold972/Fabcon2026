## About

I´m the founder of a professional company specialized in SQL Server that help companies to achieve a better database infrastructure and experience. I'm a specialist in SQL Server Database Administration and Business Intelligence projects to businesses of all sizes.
I hold a bachelor’s degree in computing science and more than 15 years of experience in the field with multiple awards, including the MVP - Microsoft Most Valuable Professional since 2015 and the PASSion Award awarded by PASS in 2017. I also hold the MCSE – Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert and the MCT – Microsoft Certified Trainer credentials since 2006.
I was born and raised in south region of Brazil and I have learned English and Spanish in school and also because of the proximity of countries like Uruguay, Argentina and Paraguay.
