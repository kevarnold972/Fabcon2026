## About

Anu, a Senior Program Manager at Microsoft, boasts over 14 years of extensive experience in the IT industry. Prior to Microsoft, she worked in Google as Senior Cloud Engineer. Her expertise spans leading Big Data projects, orchestrating Hadoop and Spark cloud migrations, and empowering large enterprise and strategic clients to harness the full potential of data and analytics through diverse Big Data technologies. Anu is also co-authoring a book titled "Dataproc Cookbook" for O'Reilly. Beyond her professional endeavors, she finds fulfillment in community engagement, dedicating time to mentor aspiring professionals and women in technology.
