## About

Mukesh leads an engineering org within Azure Data that helps Microsoft Customers & Partners adopt Azure Data and SQL Platform. His focus is to accelerate and unblock complex modernizations from Mainframe, Oracle, Teradata, Netezza, OSS, SQL sources to Azure and Data Platform.

Acceleration in revenue through these non-linear migration patterns along with bringing product truth back to engineering is his primary responsibility. He is leading a team of highly skilled product engineers, Data Platform Solutions Architects and ring-fenced Azure/MS data platform MVPs to provide technical thought leadership during customer's complex digital transformation journey.
