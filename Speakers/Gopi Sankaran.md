## About

Gopinath Sankaran is the Vice President Strategic Ecosystems at Informatica with a single-minded focus on helping customers and cloud ecosystem partners realize value with Informatica’s industry leading AI-powered Intelligent Data Management Cloud. Passionate about partnerships with cloud ecosystems and inspiring teams to drive customer delight by solving critical business problems, Gopi has been instrumental in Informatica’s GTM and Cloud Solutions strategy with cloud ecosystem partners like AWS, Microsoft, Google Cloud, Databricks, Snowflake and MongoDB. Prior to informatica, Gopi had a key leadership role in Microsoft’s Partner organization that built a transformational partner ecosystem which drove a world class partner experience with Data, AI, Analytics and Business Applications software and solution partners. Prior to Microsoft, Gopi as Head of Global Alliances at MySQL Inc played a key role in partner strategy and industrializing GTM offerings that took the company to a $1B acquisition by Sun Microsystems. Quite accustomed to entrepreneurship, Gopi was also the co-founder of open-source technology solutions company that innovated and addressed customer pain-points in life-sciences arena. 
 
Gopi earned a Master of Business Administration from University of Washington and a Master of Science in Computer Science from Emory University, Atlanta
