## About

Mario is an Engineer with 20 years of experience in the tech industry, having joined Microsoft after completing his master's degree in Distributed Systems. Mario is currently a Software Engineer Manager for the Data Factory team, having been on the team since its inception. He currently owns the Apache Airflow job in Microsoft Fabric and he's helping to bridge the gap between the Fabric SaaS experience and the Open Source Airflow community.
