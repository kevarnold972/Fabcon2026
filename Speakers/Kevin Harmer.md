## About

Kevin Harmer is the Chief Cloud Officer with over 30 years of experience in enterprise data platforms, cloud, and AI-enabled analytics. He has led 100+ large-scale initiatives across MDW, EDW, data engineering, and advanced analytics.

An expert in Azure public, private, and hybrid cloud, Kevin has delivered programs up to $50M, supporting data estates exceeding 125 TB. His focus is on building trusted, scalable data foundations that enable AI, machine learning, and agent-ready solutions at enterprise scale. Kevin leads strategy, architecture, and delivery, guiding teams of up to 20 data and AI specialists from discovery through production, helping organizations turn data into measurable business value.
