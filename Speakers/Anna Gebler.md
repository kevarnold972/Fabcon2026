## About

After completing her PhD in theoretical physics, Anna embarked on a data governance journey with Lufthansa Group. As a Senior Manager Data Quality and Governance, Anna is responsible for the configuration, population, and rollout of the Lufthansa Group Data Catalog as well as for the general rollout of data governance processes and policies throughout all of the organization's subsidiaries.
