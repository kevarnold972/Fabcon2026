## About

For over a decade, Jared has been a data engineering consultant implementing Microsoft products. For more than three decades, he has been honing his skills in performing arts. As a speaker, Jared marries these two disciplines together, creating dynamic presentations that add entertainment to education.

As a speaker, Jared has spoken at events such as:
-FABCON Vegas
-DataCon Seattle
-TechCon Dallas
-PASS Summit
-SQL Saturdays, Multiple
-Microsoft Fabric Global Online Conference
-Future Data Driven Summit
-GroupBy Conference
-and more!

He also runs the YouTube channel DataBard, focused on helping data professionals use tools efficiently.

Technically-speaking, Jared has multiple Microsoft certifications. He has extensive knowledge in:
-Microsoft Fabric
-T-SQL
-SSMS Copilot
-ETL design patterns
-Azure data solutions

Upon request, he would be happy to assist with more theatrical portions of events, such as performing musical numbers or fun demos.
