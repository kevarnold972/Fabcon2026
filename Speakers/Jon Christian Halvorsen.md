## About

Jon Christian Halvorsen is a senior data and analytics consultant at twoday, helping teams modernize how they land and model data in Microsoft Fabric. He’s spent years debugging pipelines that “almost worked” and now advocates for practical, scalable solutions that just do. A regular community speaker and Fabric early adopter, Jon Christian blends deep technical insight with hard-won lessons from production projects - always with a bit of opinion and a lot of code.
