## About

Jim Hill is a Principal Business Intelligence Engineer at BambooHR and a seasoned BI leader with deep expertise in Microsoft data technologies. He has led analytics strategy, modern data architecture, and enterprise reporting initiatives across industries including financial services, e-commerce, healthcare, education, and government. Previously, Jim served as Business Intelligence Practice Manager at JourneyTEAM, where he guided organizations in deploying Power BI, data governance, and data warehouse solutions. His earlier leadership roles at 1-800 CONTACTS, On Semiconductors, Motorola, and DHL focused on enabling data-driven decision-making through scalable BI platforms, real-time data integration, and impactful analytics solutions. He is passionate about BI best practices, data visualization, and advancing modern analytics capabilities with Microsoft Fabric.
