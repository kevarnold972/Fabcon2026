## About

Kati Iceva is the Group Engineering Manager of the Real-Time Intelligence Unified Experiences team at Microsoft, leading efforts on Real-Time Hub, Activator, AI capabilities, and integrations across Fabric and other Microsoft teams.
Previously, she led Azure Stream Analytics, Microsoft's mission-critical real-time analytics service, recognized as a market leader by Forrester. Her contributions also extend to data technologies, including multiple versions of the .NET Entity Framework and Power Query.
