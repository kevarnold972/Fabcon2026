## About

Josh Caplan is the Partner Director of Product for Microsoft OneLake in Fabric. His team focuses of defining and delivering a single SAS data lake as a service for the entire organization. Prior to his role at OneLake, Josh played a pivotal role in shaping Power BI, leading the development of developer experiences, Power BI Premium, SQL Server Analysis Services, and Azure Analysis Services. Josh also spent several years on the Bing team, where he leveraged his expertise in business intelligence to drive insights from Bing's massive data.
