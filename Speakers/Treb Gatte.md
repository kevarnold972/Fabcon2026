## About

Treb is a Power BI MVP and business intelligence expert with 24 years of experience.

Prior to becoming CEO of Marquee Insights, he worked in leadership positions at Microsoft, Starbucks, Wachovia (now Wells Fargo). He has been recognized as Data Platform MVP from Microsoft.

Treb lives in the Seattle area with his wife, children and corgi and holds an MBA from Wake Forest University.
