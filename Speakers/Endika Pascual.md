## About

Endika Pascual works with organizations to modernize their data platforms and derive greater value from their information assets. At WhereScape, he helps customers design and implement Warehouse and Lakehouse architectures that integrate advanced automation with strong governance, performance, and scalability.

He works extensively with Microsoft Fabric, leveraging Data Factory for orchestration and ingestion, Purview for metadata-driven governance, and OneLake for unified storage and sharing. His expertise includes optimizing data workloads through partitioning strategies, Delta tables, and medallion-layered architectures, ensuring efficient processing and reliable performance at scale. Endika has shared customer scenarios at industry events including FabCon and Big Data & AI World in London, demonstrating practical applications of data modeling methodologies such as Data Vault, Inmon-style enterprise warehousing, and dimensional models.
