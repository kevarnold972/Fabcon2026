## About

Abhishek is a Principal PM Manager at Microsoft, specializing in the Data Integration stack within Microsoft Fabric, particularly Data Factory. With a rich background as a Consultant and Technical Evangelist at Microsoft, Abhishek has extensive experience in Azure technologies, including IoT, Big Data, and PaaS services. He has played a pivotal role in assisting Partners and ISVs in architecting scalable applications on Azure, leveraging his deep technical expertise and strategic insights.
