## About

Tim Ward is the CEO and Co-Founder of CluedIn. With a background in software engineering, Tim spent his early career developing digital content management solutions that allowed non-technical users to create meaningful and engaging online brand experiences. In 2015 Tim, along with CluedIn’s CTO and CPO, founded CluedIn to solve the fundamental problem of how to equip business users to prepare data to deliver the insights that enterprises need to be successful. Now, CluedIn pioneers trusted AI agents in charge of data operations at scale, safely, transparently, and under full governance, so data stays clean, compliant and ready for the business at all times.

Under Tim’s leadership, CluedIn has delivered impressive year-on-year growth, been recognised for innovation, secured Series A funding and experienced significant analyst praise. He is a regular speaker on all things related to enterprise data management, AI and enjoys spirited discussions on how to move the industry forward.
