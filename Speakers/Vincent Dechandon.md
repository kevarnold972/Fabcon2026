## About

Vincent leads Product and Marketing at Galigeo, the Where Company. With 12+ years of experience in Location Intelligence, GIS, Remote Sensing and Analytics, he has helped hundreds of customers and partners turn complex location-driven use cases into measurable business outcomes. He works closely with leading technology partners such as Microsoft, SAP, IBM and Salesforce, and with major customers including GE Healthcare, Air Liquide, TotalEnergies, the French Police and Walmart. He has deep expertise in the BI ecosystem: particularly Power BI and the broader Microsoft Fabric suite ; as well as the GIS ecosystem, from private solutions like Esri to open source (GeoServer, QGIS ...).
