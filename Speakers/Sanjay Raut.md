## About

Sanjay Raut is Principal Program Manager on Fabric Customer Advisory Team (aka Fabric CAT). Sanjay has been working on SQL DW /BI for more than a decade expanding from SQL DW and Synapse dedicated pool to SSAS tabular and Power BI. As a part of Fabric CAT he has been working closely with some of the big enterprises in Retail and Consumer Product Group. His recent work has been with Synapse Analyzer, the best practice report for Azure Synapse Dedicated Pool.
