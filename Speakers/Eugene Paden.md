## About

Eugene Paden is the Chief Technology Officer of Ray Business Technologies USA, where he leads enterprise digital transformation programs for Fortune 500 and global organizations across healthcare, retail, and manufacturing. With over two decades of experience in data engineering, cloud platforms, and AI adoption, he has architected and delivered large-scale data platforms built on the Microsoft Fabric Medallion Architecture. Eugene specializes in metadata-driven transformation frameworks, enterprise data governance, and accelerating time-to-insight for business users. A frequent advisor to CIOs and technology leaders, he is passionate about helping enterprises modernize their analytics landscape and unlock the value of their data through scalable, future-ready solutions.
