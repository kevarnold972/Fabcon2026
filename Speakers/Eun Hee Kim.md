## About

Eun Hee Kim is a Product Manager for the Power BI Product Team working on Power BI Copilot. She has spent 9 years in the BI space working across different roles in data analytics, customer engagement and product management. Her experience as both a BI user and as a product manager brings a unique perspective to share BI stories with the Fabric community. Outside of work, she enjoys cooking, baking, yoga and obsessing over her dachshund.
