## About

Omer started as Software Developer in the sector in 2003 and met SQL Server in the following years and worked as SQL Server DBA since 2006. He is currently working as the IT Manager of a private company. 
Omer is also online course instructor on a lot of online educational portal such as Udemy, Turkcell Gelecegi Yazanlar, BTK Academy.
He has more than 300.000 students from 107 countries of the world.
Also he is a member of many IT Communities and he works for educational purposes in IT Industry.
Omer spoke at two Tedx events, went to more than 40 universities as a speaker.
