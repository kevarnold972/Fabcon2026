## About

Executive leader, Data, AI, and Governance strategist, and analytics and integration architect. 

For thirty years, has developed key customer relationships, led data and AI governance and strategy for those customers, led successful services programs, built practice capabilities, and mentored and developed colleagues. Utilizing a broad variety of data-focused strategic and technical skills and methodologies, he has specialized in MS Azure OpenAI, Fabric, Databricks, Oracle, Teradata, and other data and analytics platforms.

He is adept at understanding, envisioning, designing, and fully realizing the most complex systems, combining pure computer science background with decades of business experience across many industries. Passionate about understanding the client's needs and providing and executing on strategic technical roadmaps that are focused on business value.
