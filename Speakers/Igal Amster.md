## About

Igal Amster is Group Product Manager for Microsoft Fabric Graph. He leads the AI-first, enterprise-grade knowledge-graph service built natively on Fabric, eliminating ETL and scaling to hundreds of billions of edges. His team empowers GenAI agents and business users to unlock connected data insights. Previously, Igal drove other GenAI related products and platforms such as Copilot Studio and Copilot for Sales and served as a product manager for products like Cortana and Dynamics mobile app
