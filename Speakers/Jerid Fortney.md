## About

Jerid Fortney is a Data Architect at Insight in Tampa, Florida, with deep, hands-on experience delivering enterprise-scale data and analytics platforms. He partners with large global clients through the full lifecycle—strategy, architecture, ingestion, modeling, and reporting—to build reliable, high-performance solutions in data engineering, BI, analytics, and AI. His current work centers on Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, and Azure services such as Azure OpenAI, enabling organizations to unify data estates, apply strong governance, and accelerate insight and innovation.
