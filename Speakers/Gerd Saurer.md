## About

Gerd is an accomplished technology executive with over 13 years of experience at Microsoft. Currently, he serves as Principal Program Manager for Microsoft Fabric, where he collaborates with partners to enhance their offerings and define product strategy. Previously, Gerd led Azure Global's Europe Retail division, addressing large-scale engineering challenges with cloud technology, AI, Big Data, and IoT.
Gerd is also dedicated to education, teaching at different Universities for more than five years. His blend of deep technical expertise and strategic leadership makes him a sought-after speaker in the tech industry, contributing significantly to advancements in cloud computing, AI, and IoT.
