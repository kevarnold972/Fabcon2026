## About

Lance is an IT professional with over 15 years of experience, including 10 as a product leader. At Microsoft, he's a Senior Product Manager in Azure Databases working on performance monitoring solutions across the Azure SQL and Fabric SQL portfolios. He also works on Azure Arc, helping to bring Azure cloud management capabilities to on-prem and multi-cloud enviornments.

Prior to Microsoft, Lance spent over 10 years building data-centric B2B SaaS products both as a software engineer and as a product leader.
