## About

Naveen Samba is a Principal Engineering Architect Manager in the Azure Database Engineering team at Microsoft, specializing in the migration and modernization of mission-critical workloads to Azure SQL services. With more than 25 years of deep SQL Server experience, he partners with customers and ISVs to design and deliver scalable, resilient, and future-ready data platforms on Azure, enabling seamless adoption of Azure Data Services throughout their modernization journey.
