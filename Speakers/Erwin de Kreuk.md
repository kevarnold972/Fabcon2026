## About

Erwin de Kreuk is a passionate and seasoned Technology Leader in Data & AI, serving as Principal Consultant and Lead Data & AI at InSpark.

With 16 years of experience on the Microsoft Data Platform and 8 years focused on Azure, Erwin specializes in solving complex technical challenges and guiding customers in maximizing value from advanced analytics environments.

A recognized Microsoft Data Platform MVP(Fabric and Purview) and frequent speaker at national and international events, he leads a team of experts and serves on InSpark’s Technology Board. Erwin is deeply engaged in Microsoft Purview and Microsoft Fabric solutions, and plays a key role in developing InSpark’s Nitrogen Control Center, a native Fabric-based solution for seamless data integration and processing.
