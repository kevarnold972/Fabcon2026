## About

Eric Reyhle is a seasoned financial technology professional with over 25 years experience in various areas of the industry, including Business Reporting, Market Data, Enterprise Data Management, B2B payments, Source2Pay, and Treasury Management Systems. He is currently the Senior Vice President, Global Alliances at Acterys, which brings the most elegant Write-back and Workflow enhancements to Power BI. This empowers clients to actively collaborate across reports and even develop business applications that specifically address their operations.
