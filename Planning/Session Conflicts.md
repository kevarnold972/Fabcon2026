## Wednesday Time Slots

### 8:30 AM - 9:30 AM

```dataview
TABLE WITHOUT ID
  file.link as "Session",
  track as "Track",
  room as "Room",
  interest as "Interest",
  status as "Status"
FROM "Fabcon 2026/Sessions"
WHERE day = "Wednesday" AND start_time = "8:30 AM"
SORT interest DESC
```
